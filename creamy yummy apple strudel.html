Creamy Apple Strudel

Creamy Apple Strudels are a delightful dessert made with apples, cinnamon, sugar, cream cheese, and puff pastry. The apples are cooked down with sugar and cinnamon, and then mixed with a creamy filling of cream cheese. The mixture is spooned onto squares of puff pastry, folded over, and baked until golden brown. The result is a sweet, buttery, and creamy dessert that is sure to satisfy any sweet tooth.

Creamy Apple Strudel Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 1 package (16 oz) of phyllo dough
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups peeled, cored and thinly sliced apples (about 2 medium apples)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Powdered sugar, for dusting
Instruction:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a bowl, mix together the sliced apples, granulated sugar, and cinnamon until well combined.

  3. In another bowl, beat together the heavy cream, cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

  4. Place one sheet of phyllo dough onto a clean surface and brush it generously with melted butter. Repeat this step with 7 more sheets of phyllo, layering each sheet on top of the other.

  5. Spread the apple mixture over the phyllo dough, leaving about an inch of space around the edges.

  6. Spoon the cream cheese mixture over the apples, spreading it out evenly.

  7. Roll the phyllo dough up tightly, tucking in the edges as you go, and place it seam-side down onto the prepared baking sheet.

  8. Brush the top of the strudel with more melted butter.

  9. Bake the strudel for 25-30 minutes, or until it is golden brown and crispy.

  10. Let the strudel cool for a few minutes before dusting it with powdered sugar.

  11. Serve the strudel warm, either on its own or with a dollop of whipped cream or vanilla ice cream on top.

  12. Enjoy your delicious creamy apple strudel!
